WGIC Gaming Awards

Welcome to the inaugural of WGIC Gaming Awards, where excellence in the gaming industry is celebrated and honored. Organized by the Warlord Gaming Industry Consortium (WGIC), this prestigious event aims to recognize the outstanding achievements, innovations, and contributions of individuals and organizations shaping the future of gaming.
